# 20231106-agile-storys

[No one wants to be a code monkey](https://world.hey.com/dhh/no-one-wants-to-be-a-code-monkey-151478dd) 

> Software developers and designers aren't happy doing assembly line work. 
Calling that work "stories" is the great con of many modern agile processes. 
**A story without a beginning, a middle, or an end is a shitty one.** 
It's more like just a scene, shot out of sequence.
